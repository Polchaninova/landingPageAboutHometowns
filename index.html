From a Cossack settlement to a major industrial, cultural and scientific center The city of Kharkiv is the administrative center of the historical and geographical region of Ukraine, which was given the name "Slobozhanshchyna". The area of modern Kharkov exceeds 300 square meters. km, and the population is 1 million 450 thousand people. The territory of the city is a hilly plain with valleys, ravines, etc. The climate is temperate continental: the average annual air temperature is +6.90, the coldest month of the year is January (-6.10), the warmest is July (+20.50 ).
